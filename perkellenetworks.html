Perkelle Networks

Perkelle Networks is a mainly Minecraft focused network.

It has been a project that has been around since 2013 founded by Ryan. I joined in 2014 which at the time was named EliteMC. At the time I was at the age of 14 so funding was an issue for the server. Me and Ryan managed secure a sponsership with a Minecraft server hosting network which is nolonger around. This was a huge deal for us because at the time we were unable to pay for a server slot.

The server has been around for a while now and we would classify it as a success. It has an average of 20-30 players online at all time and our players tend to stick around.

My job and responsabilities on the network as co-owner is community management which involves the hiring and training of staff and managing with appeals from players that have been expelled from our community due to them breaking our rules.
